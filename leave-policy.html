Leave Policy

1. Overview

This Leave Policy defines the rules, eligibility, and process for employees to apply for leave.

2. Types of Leave

  • Annual Leave
  • Sick Leave
  • Casual Leave

3. Eligibility

All confirmed employees are eligible for leave benefits as per company guidelines.

4. Leave Application Process

  • Apply through HRMS or manager approval
  • Prior approval required (except emergency cases)

5. Important Notes

- Unapproved leave may lead to deduction - Excess leave requires HR approval
